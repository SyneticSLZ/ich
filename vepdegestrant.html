Critical Manufacturing Risks:
  • Yield Risk: <10% overall yield means 100kg starting materials for 10kg API
  • Cost Impact: COGS of $500-5,000/gram vs $50-500 for traditional drugs
  • Timeline: 18-36 months for commercial scale-up (40% probability of delays)

Key Manufacturing Components

  • ER Ligand: Custom synthesis required, no commercial source
  • Linker Chemistry: Proprietary design, stability critical
  • E3 Ligase Binder: Cereblon-targeting (thalidomide derivative, controlled substance)
  • Conjugation: Multi-step process with low yields
  • Purification: Complex due to similar molecular weight impurities
