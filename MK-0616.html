Mechanism of Action

Enlicitide decanoate is a macrocyclic peptide designed as an oral PCSK9 inhibitor. Key mechanistic features:

  • Molecular design: Small cyclic peptide (~1 kDa) engineered for oral bioavailability
  • GI stability: Survives digestive enzymes through cyclization
  • Target binding: High-affinity binding to circulating PCSK9
  • Mode of action: Prevents PCSK9-LDL receptor interaction
  • PCSK9 suppression: >90% reduction (comparable to mAbs)
