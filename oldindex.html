Cholesterol Therapies Analysis

📊 Inclisiran (Leqvio®) - Reference Drug

Small-interfering RNA (siRNA) therapy targeting PCSK9 mRNA. Approved in EU (2020) and US (2021). Unique twice-yearly dosing after initial doses. Achieves ~50% LDL reduction. ORION-4 cardiovascular outcomes trial ongoing (results expected 2026). Currently positioned as add-on therapy for high-risk patients not at LDL goal despite maximally tolerated statin therapy.

Market Access & Payer Considerations

Typical Step Therapy Sequence:

1. Maximally tolerated statin (generic, ~$5-10/month)
2. Add ezetimibe (generic, ~$10-20/month)
3. Consider bempedoic acid (if statin intolerant, ~$400/month)
4. PCSK9 inhibitor consideration (if LDL still >70-100 mg/dL)
5. Choice between: Inclisiran (2x/year), mAbs (monthly/biweekly), or oral PCSK9i (when available)
